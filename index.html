Undertale: The game where you don't have to kill anyone!

What is undertale?

Undertale is an 8-bit RPG released in 2015 by Toby Fox that tells the story of a human that falls to an underground filled with monsters. Having a great world to explore, characters with interesting personalities, a great soundtrack for every situation you are in makes this game already a great experience. But the selling point of this game is its innovative combat system where you can decide how to approach each battle. All your decisions in and outside of battle will change the story in various ways like your relationship with the creatures of the underground, what areas you can access and most importantly, which ending you get. Having 3 main routes of where your story can go but more than 50 variants of the endings.
So the main question is: How are you going to play the game?
