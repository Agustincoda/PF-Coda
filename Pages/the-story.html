Welcome to the underground!
How was the fall?

Long ago, a war erupted between humans and monsters, leading to the monsters' banishment underground under a mountain in Mount Ebbot. The humans used magic to make a barrier that sealed them away and could only be broken by the power of seven human souls.
By the time the game begins six humans have fallen and their souls have been captured. When the seventh human falls into the underground they have one objective only, to reach the king and escape. How they do this is up to you.
On your journey you will meet monsters like Toriel,Sans and Papyrus that are more friendly to you and will try to help you on your journey (that is, if you are a good person) and more aggressive ones like flowey,Undyne and Mettaton that will try to defeat you and take your soul, all in efforts to escape the underground.
All this interactions will happen in the various zones of the underground, those being:

  • The ruins
  • Snowdin
  • Waterfall
  • Hotland
  • The royal palace
  • And More!
