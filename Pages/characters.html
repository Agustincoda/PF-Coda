Meet the characters!

Frisk

The human that has fallen to the underground and has to reach the king so they can escape back to the surface.Frisk is just a kid that wears blue shorts and blue t-shirt with purple stripes. We don't know a lot about Frisk but if they went to Mount Ebott maybe there was a reason to escape from something. Some things they like is to flirt with everyone and just being silly, they are a good kid.

They may not speak much but they can ACT.

Flowey

The first monster that you encounter in the underground that has the apperance of a flower with golden petals. Flowey is the result of the experiment made by Alphys where she injected determination into a flower, thus giving life to the flower.
Flowey on the surface has an up beat attitude and a smile but he can't feel anything because he has no soul, so his real self is just evil without any care for anyone or anything other than himself.

-"In this world, it is kill or be killed"

Napstablook

Napstablook (also known as blooky) is a ghost that has an almost depressed attitude and doesn't like to talk withthe other monsters. He is the cousin of Mettaton and he has musical talent as good the star of the underground.

-"i usually come to the RUINS because there's nobody around... but today i met somebody nice...."

Toriel

The former queen of the underground. She left the castle and started living in the ruins after the death of her childs Asriel and Chara. Toriel has a motherly attitude towards the humans that have fallen in the underground during the years and she offered all them to live together, but all refused and went to face the king Asgore where they met their fate.
In the recent years Toriel went to the exit of the ruins where she has conversations with an unknow monster, mostly to tell jokes to each other. During one of this talks she made the unknow monster promise to help the next human that gets out of the ruins and keep them safe.

-"What a terrible creature, torturing such a poor, innocent youth... Ah, do not be afraid, my child"

Sans

Sans the skeleton is a resident in Snowdin that lives with his brother Papyrus. He is a guy that likes to relax and do nothing as much as he can, but he also likes to tell jokes a lot. Sans joined the royal guard because his brother wanted to be part of them, and he didn't want to left his Papyrus with that kind of job alone (even though he doesn't like to work).
Sans gets around with all the monsters in Snowdin and seems to be on good terms with the royal scientist Alphys and the head of the royal guard Undyne.

-"Do you think even the worst person can change?"

Papyrus

The great Papyrus! A squeleton that wants to make a name for himself. He enlisted in the royal guard and wants to go up the ranks so that all can know his name and be friends with everyone. He lives with his older brother Sans in the town of Snowdin and although he loves his brother he can't stand his laziness and all of his puns.
Papyrus is an innocent soul that likes spaghetti and make puzzles but his number one objective is to capture a human.

-"JUST DO WHAT THE GREAT PAPYRUS WOULD DO! BELIEVE IN YOU!"

Undyne

Undyne is the head of the royal guard and is under the command of the king with the task of find the next human that falls in the underground, capture them and take their soul. She lives in the acuatic zone of Waterfall near the house of Napstablook.
All this responsibility makes it seem that Undyne only has place in her life for battle and war, but she has a kind soul and worries for the lives of everyone. She is friends with Sans and Papyrus and the latter often goes to her house to cook and train.

-"You're Gonna Have To Try A Little Harder Than THAT."

Alphys

The brilliant doctor Alphys that worked hard to be THE royal scientist, although she may have cheated to get where she is. She lives in the lab in hotland mostly by herself where she watches anime and (sometimes) makes experiments.
She is a shy person in person but in the social media of the underground she is more expresive than Papyrus. She is friends with the king,Sans and Papyrus, seems to know Mettaton and has a "friendship" with Undyne.

-"S-so, ahhh, now I want to help you! Using my knowledge, I can easily guide you through Hotland! I know a way right to ASGORE's castle, no problem!"

Mettaton

The Star of the underground. Mettaton is a ghost that possesses a robot made by Alphys that needed a power source and because she couldn't made one, mettaton was that power source.
Mettaton agreed to that deal beacuse he wanted to be a big name in the underground and that body was the way to get there. After achieving fame he had tons of shows and is the owner of a hotel located in the entrance of the core.
Besides knowing Alphys, he is the cousin of Napstablook although they don't see each other as often. Also Papyrus has a crush on Mettaton... how could that go down?

-"SORRY, FOLKS! THE OLD PROGRAM'S BEEN CANCELLED! BUT WE'VE GOT A FINALE THAT'LL DRIVE YOU WILD! REAL ACTION! REAL DRAMA! REAL BLOODSHED! ON OUR NEW SHOW ... ATTACK OF THE KILLER ROBOT!"

Asgore

The king of all monsters. After they were banished Asgore didn't want to go to war but the death of both Asriel, his son and Chara, his adopted children made Asgore go into a war against humans, killing anyone who falls into the underground. His wife Toriel abandon him and considers him a monster beacuse of what he wants to do to the humans in the surface.
Despite all of this Asgore is a nice person and is friendly with monsters and humans, but his duty comes first. He is the mentor of Undyne and is the superior of doctor Alphys but is in friendly terms with both of them.

-"I so badly want to say, 'would you like a cup of tea?' But... you know how it is."

Asriel

Asriel, the prince of the monsters. Being raised by his parents Asgore and Toriel, Asriel had a pretty good childhood and when Chara fell to the underground he had a sibling to spend the time with.
But after the accident occurred, Chara died and he was force to absorb their soul and take their body to Charas village and bury it in there. When Asriel arrived, the humans attacked him but he refused to fight back even though Chara's soul wanted to kill the humans. Being fataly wounded, Asriel returned to the underground and died on top of some golden flowers.

-"Oh, and Frisk... Be careful in the outside world, OK? Despite what everyone thinks, it's not as nice as it is here. There are a lot of Floweys out there."

Chara

The first human that fell in the underground after the barrier was cast. They are not fond of humans but we don't know why. we know that living with Asriel and his parents wasn't enough to let that hatred die. So they made a plan were they would kill themselves and make Asriel absorb their soul. All of this so Asriel could cross the barrier and exterminate the humans. But they didn't count that Asriel wouldn't fight back and so the plan had failed.
Their souls disappeared and only needed someone with enough determination so Chara could come back again.

-"Let us erase this pointless world, and move on to the next"
